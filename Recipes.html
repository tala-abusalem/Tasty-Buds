Spaghetti Carbonara

This classic Italian pasta dish is made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.

Ingredients: Spaghetti, eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, pancetta, black pepper

Instructions: Cook spaghetti al dente, fry pancetta until crispy, mix eggs and cheese, toss everything together, season with black pepper.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious homemade cookies filled with chocolate chips.

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, butter, chocolate chips, baking soda, salt, vanilla extract

Instructions: Cream butter and sugar, add vanilla extract, mix dry ingredients, fold in chocolate chips, bake at 350°F for 10-12 minutes.

Chicken Tikka Masala

A popular Indian dish consisting of marinated chicken pieces in a spiced curry sauce.

Ingredients: Chicken, yogurt, lemon juice, spices (cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric), tomato sauce, cream

Instructions: Marinate chicken, grill until cooked, sauté onions and spices, add tomato sauce, simmer, stir in cream, add chicken pieces, cook until heated through.
